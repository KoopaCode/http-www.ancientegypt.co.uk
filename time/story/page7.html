'Follow me!' he said to Mehetra. Amenemopet raced through the rooms of the temple. He stopped in front of a carved wall and pointed to a list of cartouches.

'This problem was first noticed when they were alive,' he pointed to a line of cartouches. 'That was many years ago, and the problem is only getting worse.'

Amenemopet shook his head again. Then, he turned to Mehetra and said, 'Go now, child. I must be alone to think...' With that, Amenemopet turned and walked away.
